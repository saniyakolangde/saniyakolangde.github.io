Therapily is my capstone graduation project of my undergraduate degree which received the silver medal in our university innovation fair. Therapily is a mental health care application which uses artificial intelligence to provide users activities after detecting their emotions. The application also houses a chatbot to conduct humanistic, soothing conversations with the user. We expect to receive our IP Rights certificate from the Ministry by mid-February 2022 before proceeding to refine the app for public launch.
